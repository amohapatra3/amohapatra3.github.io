Documentation samples

In my time at Amazon Web Services (AWS), I supported documentation for Amazon ECS and AWS Cloud Map across multiple channels.

My documentation creation process for new product functionality involved reading design docs, testing the functionality, and getting reviews by engineering and product. Feature work often involved walking engineers through shared processes. In addition to feature documentation, I worked on customer feedback mostly in the form of feedback tickets that came directly from customers or indirectly via colleagues that engaged with customers directly (SAs, support engineers, etc). I also used metrics based on page views and thumbs up/down feedback. I was an engaged member of the documentation org who contributed to organizational goals such as scenario-based content refactoring and an open-source tutorial project for accessibility.
